Parts and materials used in the repair are covered under the manufacturer's or supplier's warranty. While All County Collision will

Important Paint Care Instructions

Fresh paint requires curing time. Please follow these guidelines for 45 days after the repair:

- Do NOT apply wax, sealants, ceramic coatings, or paint protection film (PPF).

- Use only clean water and mild soap for washing.

- Avoid brush-style automatic car washes.

Failure to follow these steps may void your paint warranty.

Warranty Limitations

This warranty does NOT cover:

- Misuse, neglect, or accidents post-repair.

- Environmental damage (e.g., bird droppings, tree sap).

- Untreated rust/corrosion existing at time of repair.

- Any incidental or consequential damages (e.g., rental costs, loss of value).
