1. Repair Authorization

Customer authorizes the Repair Facility to assess, diagnose, and repair the Vehicle.
Customer confirms they have the legal right to authorize these repairs. Repair Facility is
also authorized to photograph, test drive, and collect data as needed for proper repairs.

2. Payment

Customer agrees all repair costs, including parts, labor, taxes, fees, and any additional
charges such as towing or storage must be paid for in full, before release of the vehicle. A
final invoice will be provided.

3. Repair Process

a. Damage Analysis: Customer agrees to repairs based on Repair Facility’s Damage
Analysis. Any insurance estimate is for reference only and does not dictate repair
methods.

b. Additional Damage: If further damage is found, the Repair Facility will notify the
Customer and provide an updated repair plan. Repairs will proceed upon approval.

4. Insurance Involvement: Repair Facility determines the correct repair process, not the
insurance company. While the Repair Facility may communicate with insurers to assist
with payment, Customer understands that they are fully responsible for any costs not
covered by insurance.

___ (Customer Initial) I understand that my insurance may not cover all necessary
repairs, and I am financially responsible for all charges including, but not limited to
labor rate discrepancies, required labor operations, etc.

5. Possession & Payment Rights
Repair Facility retains a lien on the Vehicle for unpaid amounts and may hold the Vehicle
until payment is received. If legal action is necessary to recover costs, Customer agrees to
pay all related expenses.

___ (Customer Initial) I understand that I cannot transfer ownership of the Vehicle until
all repair costs are paid in full.

6. Cancellation & Breach of Contract

If Customer cancels repairs, Repair Facility is entitled to compensation for time, labor, and
any allocated resources, plus applicable interest.

7. Parts Selection

Repair Facility prefers OEM parts for repairs. Customer may choose to have only OEM
parts used, but must agree to pay the difference in cost, if the insurance company quotes
otherwise.

___ (Customer Initial) (IF APPLICABLE) I agree to pay the difference between what the
insurance company quotes and the price of Original Equipment Manufacture parts.

Customer acknowledges that using non-OEM parts may affect vehicle value, lease
agreements, and manufacturer warranties.

8. Workmanship Warranty

Repair Facility guarantees workmanship for the lifetime of the Vehicle while owned by
the Customer. Any defects in workmanship will be corrected at no cost, provided Repair
Facility is given the opportunity to fix the issue. This warranty covers workmanship only
and does not include parts or materials.
